A Model of Multi-dimensional Mental Health Education in Secondary Schools

Our model of mental health education is directed at the student's whole persona. Through mental health education in the classroom, as well as individual psychological counseling, we use psychological theory and related knowledge to equip students to solve the problems that encountered during their growth, and thus maintain their psychological health.

Moreover, through developing a positive outlook towards life, we expect they will become more confident in their own strength and be able to fully develop their potential. They will thereby be able to face the challenges of nowaday society and adapt to its realities.
